About

What Is Splooshed?

Splooshed provides water consumption data*  for specific food ingredients or complete recipes.

We offer two services: a water-impact calculator for individual foods and recipes and a Chrome extension that lets you see water data while browsing your favorite recipe sites.

Some Facts about California's Drought

  • California is going through its worst drought in 1200 years.
  • 80% of the amount of water available in California is used by agriculture.
  • Yet only 1% of the California's GDP is produced by agriculture.
  • 17 communities are in danger of running out of water within the next 60 to 120 days.
  • Poor families already spending 1/5th of their household budget on food will be disproportionately affected by the drought.

What We Hope to Do About It

  1. Raise awareness about the disproportionate usage of water by California's agriculture industry
  2. Make it easier to make informed decisions about food purchases
  3. Identify the most water intensive crops and key agricultural offenders

API

Hey developers! Interested in working with water impact data? Splooshed offers three open-access JSON endpoints:

  • GET http://www.splooshed.com/list_foods
    returns a list of all food names understood by Splooshed.
  • GET http://www.splooshed.com/food?inputFood=[food]&inputAmount=[amount]&inputUnit=[unit]
    returns water-impact information for a single food of the given amount.
  • POST http://www.splooshed.com/recipe
    returns water-impact information for a recipe (passed in as newline-separated text).
